ROUTES

A Mosaic of Unforgettable Experiences

Embark on an extraordinary journey through Canada, a vast nation of diverse landscapes and cultural richness. With three thoughtfully curated routes, each unveiling a unique facet of the country, you'll traverse the nergetic cityscape of Calgary, immerse yourself in the awe-inspiring beauty of Banff and Jasper National Parks, and explore the dynamic cities of the East.

Whether wandering through the urban charm of Kensington or basking in the tranquility of Emerald Lake, each day promises a blend of exploration and cultural immersion, ensuring an unforgettable Canadian experience.

EXPLORE ROUTES

Route 1

Embark on a natural adventure through the stunning Rocky Mountains. Begin by exploring the vibrant city of Calgary on the first day, immersing yourself in its urban atmosphere and enjoying the beauty of Prince's Island Park.

The next day, delve into the majesty of Banff National Park, touring the iconic Lake Moraine and relaxing in the hot springs of Upper Hot Springs. Continue to the breathtaking Lake Louise and explore Yoho National Park, culminating your journey in Jasper National Park with scenic hikes and a magical sunset at Pyramid Lake.

Route 2

Experience the rich history and vibrant urban life of the Canadian East Coast. Begin in Toronto, marvel at the imposing CN Tower, and explore the Distillery District. Then, head to the majestic Niagara Falls for a unique experience before exploring the capital, Ottawa, with its historical monuments and lively ByWard Market.

Conclude your journey in fascinating Montreal, exploring its charming Old Montreal and enjoying the culinary scene of Plateau-Mont-Royal.

Route 3

Immerse yourself in the natural beauty of British Columbia, starting in the modern city of Vancouver. Discover Stanley Park, visit Granville Island, and explore the charm of Gastown. Continue to Whistler for outdoor activities and relaxation in its spas.

Head to the stunning Pacific Rim National Park in Tofino for whale watching and pristine beaches. Finally, take a ferry to the picturesque Victoria on Vancouver Island to explore Butchart Gardens and the charming downtown.
